Who We Are

ESCA is a personality-based travel website designed to connect people with destinations that truly fit them.


Instead of recommending the same places to everyone, ESCA believes that each traveler seeks different experiences. Some people enjoy peaceful nature escapes, while others prefer cultural exploration, emotional journeys, or exciting adventures.


By connecting destinations with personality types, ESCA helps users discover places where they feel most comfortable, inspired, and connected.


What Makes ESCA Different

ESCA combines travel and personality psychology to create a more personal travel experience.


Every destination is carefully matched with personality groups such as Analysts, Diplomats, Sentinels, and Explorers.


This allows travelers to explore places that match not only their interests, but also their character and emotions.


Our Mission

Our mission is to help people travel with deeper meaning by discovering destinations that feel personally connected to who they are.

Whether you seek adventure, peace, culture, or inspiration, ESCA is here to guide you toward experiences that truly match your vibe.
